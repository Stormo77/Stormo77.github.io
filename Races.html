Races

There are many Races to choose from in D&D with some people even making their own!(similar to classes)

The classes that you can chose from are as follows The Dwarf, Elf, Halfling, Humman, Dragonborn, Gnome, Half-Elf, Half-Orc and Tiefling. Regarding the classes the only variaties come in roleplay and stats with humans getting a bonus to all of there stats overall while others focus on a specific trait and the possibility of a small bonus on another trait, and if you get good enough you can create your own Race! just make sure its balanced.
